Report Garbage

 

MarkBins, your digital solution to combat street litter and promote cleaner communities! With our platform, you can easily report garbage hotspots by uploading photos and pinpointing their exact locations with latitude and longitude coordinates. Our integrated Twitter bot ensures swift action by tagging municipal authorities for prompt cleanup. Together, let's tackle the scourge of open garbage on our streets and create a healthier environment for all. Click 'Get Started' below to begin your journey toward cleaner, greener neighborhoods.


 

Report Potholes

 

SpotFill! We're on a mission to make your commute smoother by tackling potholes head-on. With SpotFill, reporting potholes is as easy as snapping a photo and pinpointing the location. Our platform swiftly notifies authorities, including @mybmcroads, ensuring prompt repairs and safer roads for all. Potholes aren't just inconvenient; they pose serious risks to motorists, cyclists, and pedestrians, causing accidents and vehicle damage. By reporting potholes with SpotFill, you're not just addressing a nuisance; you're contributing to safer, more efficient transportation networks and fostering a better quality of life in our communities. Let's work together to fill those spots and pave the way for smoother journeys ahead!
